Low Tech-High Five!

Cicadra [シ・カ・デラ] - Insect Synthesizer

[2024] Synthesizer / Toys

Cicada-shaped synthesizer, inspired by the summer ambiance in Japan. The primary focus is on creating sounds that mimic the distinctive call of cicadas, the insects synonymous with Japanese summer. This workshop blends simple technology with artistic flair, offering a unique experience in crafting music inspired directly by nature.

From this project, a sub-project called "AMPRRRONG" was born. Through AMPRRRONG, he recorded an EP in 2020, released by Anti Trust Record under the title Salamander Kodok Amprong: BAB I – The Shell Creatures. By 2023, the exploration of Awikworks Modular had entered its fourth stage of evolution, where several modules were assembled into a single compact unit.

Awikworks Origin.

[2017] The Four Horsemen of Awikworks Modulars

Starting from an interest in building a modular synthesizer independently, this project was initially named "Modular Miskin." However, over time, the name was felt to bring misfortune, leading to its change to "Awikworks" with the motto "low tech-high five." Equipped with open-source modules, Muzeian continued to develop his DIY synthesizer. In addition to purchasing electronic components directly, he often dismantled and modified various used electronic devices to be repurposed into new instruments.

From this project, a sub-project called "AMPRRRONG" was born. Through AMPRRRONG, he recorded an EP in 2020, released by Anti Trust Record under the title Salamander Kodok Amprong: BAB I – The Shell Creatures. By 2023, the exploration of Awikworks Modular had entered its fourth stage of evolution, where several modules were assembled into a single compact unit.
